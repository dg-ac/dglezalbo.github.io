About BaseCase

BaseCase is a no-code platform for life science companies to effectively communicate product value via customized tablet and mobile apps.

My role

In 2016, I joined BaseCase as a UI Designer/Consultant. My primary responsibility was designing and implementing iPad and phone apps for prominent healthcare and life sciences companies.

BaseCase was acquired by Certara in early 2018. I continued to contribute as a Senior UI Designer until March 2020.

Works

All of the apps I developed were designed and implemented directly using the powerful BaseCase software. For certain visual elements such as backgrounds, icons, and other design components, I utilized Sketch to create custom materials.
